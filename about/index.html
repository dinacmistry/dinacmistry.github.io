About

I am a Doctoral Candidate in Physics at Northeastern University in the MOBS Lab situated within the Network Science Institute. My dissertation work has focused on complex networks and infectious disease modeling. I also have a M.Sc in Physics from Northeastern University. Previous to this I obtained my Hons. B.Sc with high distinction in Astronomy & Physics at the University of Toronto, Canada.
My research interests lie at the intersection of complex networks and data science for social good & public health.

In another life, I played the tenor and double tenor steel pans in various bands and music remains a passion of my life.
